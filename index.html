Dental Advice

1. Brush Your Teeth Twice a Day

Brushing your teeth twice a day is essential for maintaining good oral hygiene. Use fluoride toothpaste and a soft-bristled toothbrush.

2. Floss Daily

Flossing helps remove food particles and plaque from between your teeth and under the gumline where your toothbrush can't reach.

3. Limit Sugary Foods and Drinks

Consuming too much sugar can lead to tooth decay. Try to limit your intake of sugary foods and drinks.

4. Visit Your Dentist Regularly

Regular dental check-ups are important for catching and preventing problems early. Visit your dentist at least twice a year.
